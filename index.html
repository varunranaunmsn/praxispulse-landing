Common questions

Straight answers.

What are the three depths of insight? +
PraxisPulse gives you insight at three depths. The first looks inward — your own clinic's patients, marketing returns and revenue. The second looks sideways — how your clinic is doing compared to others in your specialty in your city. The third looks outward — the wider trends and global picture for your field. You start with the first straight away; the city and nationwide views open up as your clinic builds up a few months of data.
Is my patient data safe — and do you use it to train AI? +
Your data is encrypted, hosted on Indian servers, kept separate from every other clinic, and handled under India's DPDP Act, 2023. It is never sold, never shared, and never used to train any AI model. City and nationwide benchmarks are built only from anonymised, aggregated figures — never identifiable patient records.
Can PraxisPulse raise GST invoices for my clinic? +
Yes. When an appointment is completed you can generate a clean, GST-ready invoice with your clinic's details and mode of payment — a copy to print for the patient and one kept for your accountant. Every invoice is stored in one sheet you can review anytime.
How do I get my data into PraxisPulse? +
Export your data from your current software as a file — most tools do this in a couple of clicks — then upload it to PraxisPulse. The app reads it and sorts the columns out for you. Most clinics are fully set up in about 15 minutes, and our team is on WhatsApp if you'd like a hand. You can also start fresh.
I'm a GP — my patients come when unwell, not on a schedule. Is it still useful? +
Very. Beyond reminders, it keeps clean records and GST invoices, nudges chronic patients due for a diabetes, BP or thyroid review, shows your busy and quiet periods, and tells you which marketing actually brings paying patients — then unlocks city and national benchmarks for your field as you stay.
What does it cost? +
It's free. No credit card, no paywalls, no hidden charges — the full app, including the clinic tools and your own insight. You can leave anytime and export all your data with one click.
