Common questions

Straight answers.

Do I have to replace my current clinic software? +
No. PraxisPulse sits on top of whatever you use — KiVi, Allenger, Practo Ray, DocPulse or even Excel. It just needs a weekly data export. It doesn't replace your appointments or billing software.
I only want to keep patient records for now. Is it still for me? +
Absolutely. Many clinics start by simply moving off paper and Excel — patients, appointments and WhatsApp recalls in one place. The AI growth analytics are there when you're ready, not forced on you on day one.
How is this different from Practo Ray or DocPulse? +
Those are practice-management tools — they store appointments and generate bills. PraxisPulse is the intelligence layer above them: it reads that data and tells you what to do with it — who to recall, which appointments will no-show, and where revenue is leaking.
Is my patient data secure? +
Yes. Encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (HTTPS), hosted on Indian servers, each clinic isolated, and handled under India's DPDP Act, 2023. We never train AI models on your patient data.
How long does setup take? +
15–30 minutes for your first upload. After that the weekly refresh takes under a minute. Our team is on WhatsApp if you get stuck.
What does it cost? +
Start with a free 30-day trial — no credit card. You'll see clear, simple plans inside the app once you're set up, and you can cancel anytime.
