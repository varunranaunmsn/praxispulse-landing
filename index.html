Frequently asked questions

Do I need to replace my existing clinic software?
No. PraxisPulse sits on top of whatever you use — Practo Ray, DocPulse or any clinic software / Excel. We just need your weekly data export (3 CSVs). We don't replace your appointments or billing software.
How is this different from your current clinic software?
Those tools are practice management software — they store appointments, generate bills, and show static reports. PraxisPulse is not practice management. We're the layer above: we read the data those tools produce and tell you what to do with it — which patients to recall, which appointments are likely no-shows, where revenue is leaking, what's driving growth.
Is my patient data secure?
Yes. All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (HTTPS). Hosted on Indian servers (AWS Mumbai region). Each clinic's data is fully isolated. We never train AI models on patient data.
How long does setup take?
15–30 minutes for your first upload. After that, weekly refresh takes under 1 minute. Our team is on WhatsApp if you get stuck.
Which specialties does it work for?
Dental, dermatology, gynaecology, ENT, general practice, aesthetics, wellness.
Can my receptionist use it without training?
Yes — the front-desk view is designed to be self-explanatory. Most receptionists are productive on day one.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime from Settings → Billing. We give you 30 days to export all your data. No questions asked, no fees.
Do you support WhatsApp Business?
Yes — we integrate with AiSensy. You can send recall messages in Hindi or English directly from PraxisPulse.
Is there a mobile app?
PraxisPulse works as a Progressive Web App — open it in Chrome on your phone, tap "Add to Home Screen," and it behaves like a native app.
