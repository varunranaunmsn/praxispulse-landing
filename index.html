Common questions

Straight answers.

Do I need to replace my existing clinic software? +
No. PraxisPulse sits on top of whatever you use — KiVi, Allenger, Practo Ray, DocPulse, or even Excel. We just need a weekly data export. We don't replace your appointments or billing software.
How is this different from Practo Ray or DocPulse? +
Those are practice management tools — they store appointments, generate bills, and show static reports. PraxisPulse is the intelligence layer above them: we read the data those tools produce and tell you what to do with it — which patients to recall, which appointments are likely no-shows, where revenue is leaking, what's driving growth.
Is my patient data secure? +
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (HTTPS). Hosted on Indian servers (AWS Mumbai region). Each clinic's data is fully isolated. We never train AI models on your patient data.
How long does setup take? +
15–30 minutes for your first upload. After that, the weekly refresh takes under 1 minute. Our team is on WhatsApp if you get stuck at any point.
Which specialties does it work for? +
Dental, dermatology, gynaecology, ENT, general practice, aesthetics, and wellness. The analytics adapt to your patient flow patterns regardless of specialty.
Can my receptionist use it without training? +
Yes — the front-desk view is designed to be self-explanatory. Most receptionists are productive on day one. The owner dashboard is more detailed but equally intuitive.
Do you support WhatsApp Business? +
Yes — we integrate with AiSensy. You can send recall messages in Hindi or English directly from PraxisPulse using your own WhatsApp Business number.
What if I want to cancel? +
Cancel anytime from Settings → Billing. You get 30 days to export all your data. No questions asked, no fees.
