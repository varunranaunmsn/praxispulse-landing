Common questions

Straight answers.

Is PraxisPulse really built only for dermatology & aesthetics? +

Yes. We removed everything generic. Packages and sessions, before & after photography, treatment-wise revenue, recall for repeat courses — the product is shaped around how a skin clinic earns, whether you run medical dermatology, aesthetics, or both.

How do before & after photos work — and are they safe? +

Upload photos to a patient's timeline at each sitting and compare side-by-side in the chair. Photos are stored encrypted on Indian servers, consent-first under the DPDP Act, 2023, visible only to your clinic, and used to train AI only with your consent. Delete them anytime.

What does the AI actually do? +

It analyses your clinic's own data and answers in plain English: your top-earning treatments, patients due to return, seasonal peaks, marketing that pays back. As your history builds, it benchmarks you against your city and your field. Photo-based AI skin analysis for consultations is in early access.

Can it track my laser and PRP packages? +

Yes — that's a core feature in early access. Define a course (say, 6 laser sessions), and PraxisPulse tracks sessions done, sessions left, and who's gone quiet mid-package, with a one-tap WhatsApp nudge to bring them back.

How do I move from my current software or Excel? +

Export your data as a file — most tools do this in two clicks — and upload it. PraxisPulse sorts the columns out for you, flags quality issues, and most clinics are set up in about 15 minutes. Our team is on WhatsApp if you'd like a hand.

What does it cost? +

We're in an early-bird phase for founding clinics, with preferred terms for early adopters — including full AI insight. There's no credit card to get started, and you can leave anytime and export all your data in one click. Message us on WhatsApp for current early-bird details.

Can it handle my Instagram & WhatsApp enquiries? +

Yes — that's PraxisPulse Connect. WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, JustDial and Practo enquiries come into one inbox, an AI front desk drafts each reply for you to approve, and a simple pipeline moves every enquiry from new to consult-booked (which adds the patient to your records automatically). WhatsApp and Instagram channels roll out as we complete Meta's approval; you can try the full flow today with demo data.

Do you offer AI skin analysis from photos? +

It's in early access. The world's most advanced clinics now open consultations with an AI photo scan — the patient sees their own skin scored for pigmentation, texture, pores and acne, which leads naturally into a treatment plan. PraxisPulse is bringing that same clinical-grade analysis to any phone or tablet — no dedicated skin-analysis machine — with every photo stored consent-first under the DPDP Act, 2023.
