Common questions

Straight answers.

How do I get my data into PraxisPulse? +
Export a spreadsheet (CSV) from your current software — most clinic tools and Excel do this in a couple of clicks — then drag it into PraxisPulse. The app reads it and sorts the columns out for you. Most clinics are fully set up in about 15 minutes, and our team is on WhatsApp if you'd like a hand.
Can I keep using my current software too? +
Yes — keep working in KiVi, Allenger, Practo Ray, DocPulse or Excel exactly as you do today. Whenever you want a clearer picture, upload your latest export and PraxisPulse turns it into records, reminders and insight. No switching, no migration, no risk.
I'm a GP — my patients come when they're unwell, not on a schedule. Is it still useful? +
Very. Beyond check-up reminders, PraxisPulse keeps clean records, nudges chronic patients due for a diabetes, BP or thyroid review, shows your busy and quiet periods so you can plan staff, and tells you which marketing actually brings paying patients. The recall reminders are optional — the records and insight help every clinic.
I only want to keep patient records for now. Is that okay? +
Absolutely. Many clinics start by simply moving off paper and Excel — patients, appointments and WhatsApp reminders in one calm place. The growth insight is there for the day you want it, never forced on you.
Is my patient data safe? +
Yes. Your data is encrypted, hosted on Indian servers, kept separate from every other clinic, and handled under India's DPDP Act, 2023. It's yours — we never sell or share it, and never use it to train AI.
What does it cost? +
Start with a free trial — no credit card needed. You'll see simple, clear plans inside the app once you're set up, and you can cancel anytime.
