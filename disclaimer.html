Not Medical Advice

PraxisPulse is a business intelligence and operational analytics platform built for independent clinic owners in India. It helps clinic doctors understand their practice's financial health, patient flow trends, and operational efficiency.

PraxisPulse does not:

  • Provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations
  • Analyse patient symptoms or clinical data for health outcomes
  • Replace clinical judgment or the advice of a licensed healthcare professional
  • Function as a clinical decision support system, EMR, or regulated medical device

All outputs from PraxisPulse — including AI predictions, analytics, and patient segmentation — are derived from operational and financial data (appointments, revenue, visit frequency). They reflect business patterns only. They must not be interpreted as clinical assessments.

No Clinical Reliance

Clinic doctors using PraxisPulse must exercise independent clinical judgment. PraxisPulse insights — such as patient churn risk or no-show predictions — are operational indicators to guide practice management, not patient care decisions. Never use PraxisPulse output to decide on a patient's diagnosis, medication, referral, or treatment plan.

PraxisPulse analytics are only as accurate as the data uploaded by the clinic. We do not verify the accuracy, completeness, or clinical validity of uploaded data. We are not responsible for business decisions made on the basis of inaccurate input data.

WhatsApp Automation

PraxisPulse's WhatsApp automation features send appointment reminders, recall messages, and practice communications on behalf of your clinic. These are administrative communications only. They are not treatment instructions, clinical follow-up advice, or medical guidance of any kind.
